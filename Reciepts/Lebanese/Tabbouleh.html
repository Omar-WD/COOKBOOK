Lebanese Tabbouleh

This is an easy healthy recipe for a delicious and authentic Lebanese Tabbouleh salad. Serve this parsley salad on it's own as a light meal or as a side dish to grilled meats

1. Combine parsley, mint, scallions, tomatoes and bulgur in a medium bowl.
2. Add olive oil, lemon juice, salt and pepper, (and other spices if using) mix well, taste and adjust seasoning to your taste.
3. Serve immediately or refrigerate if serving later
