Patbingsoo

A blend of shaved ice with sweet red beans
Korean Soul Food
Most popular summer dessert
TOTAL
20 mins
# Ingredient Direction
400 g Ice cubes Put the ice blocks into a food processor until it has a smooth texture.
Red bean 4 Tbsp Place the shaved ice into a chilled serving bowl. Top up with your choice of fruit, red bean paste, mochi.
2 Tbsp Condensed milk Garnish with sweetened condensed milk.
