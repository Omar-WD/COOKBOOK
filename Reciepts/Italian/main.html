Ravioli with ricotta and lemon

in cherry tomato sauce

Author:

Fred Egd

Prep.Time:

30 min

Ingredients:

FOR THE PASTA

2 cups
Flour
3 large
Eggs
1/2 teaspoon
Salt
1 spoon
olive oil

FOR THE FILLING

1 cup
ricotta cheese
1
Lemon, zested
a pinch
salt and pepper

FOR THE SAUCE

1 ts
olive oil
2
garlic cloves, minced
2 cups
cherry tomatoes
1/2 cup
vegetable broth
a pinch
salt and pepper
40 gr
fresh basil
50 gr
parmesan cheese, grated

Instructions:

1

In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Create a well in the center and add the eggs and olive oil. Using a fork, whisk the eggs and slowly incorporate the flour until a dough forms. Knead the dough for about 5 minutes until it is smooth and elastic. Cover the dough with plastic wrap and let it rest for 30 minutes.

2

In a separate mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, lemon zest, salt, and pepper to create the filling. Mix well and set aside.

3

After the dough has rested, cut it into four equal parts. Using a pasta roller or a rolling pin, roll out each piece into a thin sheet. Place teaspoons of the ricotta and lemon filling on one sheet of pasta, leaving about 1 inch of space between each mound of filling.

4

Place the second sheet of pasta on top of the first, pressing down around the filling to seal the edges of the ravioli. Cut the ravioli apart using a knife or a pastry cutter. Repeat with the remaining dough and filling.

5

In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add garlic and saut√© until fragrant, about 1 minute.

6

Add cherry tomatoes to the skillet and cook until they start to burst and release their juices, stirring occasionally. This should take about 5-7 minutes.

7

Add broth to the skillet, and stir to combine. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

8

Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the ravioli and cook for 2-3 minutes until they float to the surface.

9

Using a slotted spoon, transfer the ravioli to the skillet with the cherry tomato sauce. Gently toss the ravioli in the sauce until they are fully coated.

10

Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves and grated Parmesan cheese.

Enjoy your homemade ravioli filled with ricotta and lemon in cherry tomato sauce!
