# Ingredient Direction
1 Pack Cabbage Slice the cabbage into 2.5cm strips. Tip into a bowl, mix with 1 tbsp sea salt, then set aside for 1 hr.
2 Cups Korean Radish Transfer to a large bowl and toss through the paste, along with the radishes, carrot and spring onions.
2 Tbsp Chilli Powder Serve straight away or pack into a large jar, seal and leave to ferment at room temperature overnight.
